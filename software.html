Bricklayer: the ultimate DNA assembly tool

The BrickLayer software is a web-based application that allows teams to virtually engineer their DNA constructs and design primers for the construction of their engineered parts - all in one place. Users can query and select parts from the BioBrick database, and design a construct with their chosen parts.
